Hukkaputki
"It's above and beyond what we require." Dr David Millard



Hukkaputki is a text based adventure game.

The game offers visual novel adventure but also stimulates personal reflection on topics such as various substances, mental health themes, a parent's alcohol use, doping, and online harassment. As a player, you can influence the course of the game by making different choices in familiar and everyday situations. So it's worth playing the game several times with different choices!
